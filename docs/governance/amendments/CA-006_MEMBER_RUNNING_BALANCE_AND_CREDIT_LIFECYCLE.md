# CA‑006 · Constitutional Amendment — Continuous Member Running Balance & Credit Lifecycle

**Amendment ID:** CA‑006 · **Status:** **RATIFIED & FROZEN** (owner, OD‑06, 2026‑07‑23)
**Depends on:** CA‑001, CA‑002. **Baseline:** `main` @ `6adb118`.

---

## 1 · Rules

### 1.1 · Continuous member running balance
Each member owns **one continuous running balance** for their entire lifetime in the system. It is
**never reset by fiscal‑year boundaries**. Annual subscription assessments **increase** the outstanding
balance; payments **reduce** it; a positive credit is **automatically consumed** by future subscription
assessments (per OD‑02 / CA‑002).

### 1.2 · Credit is future purchasing power
A member credit represents **future purchasing power inside the member account**, **not** a temporary
refundable balance. It **never expires**. (Refunds reverse the **origin receipt** per OD‑05/CA‑005 — they
do not refund the credit as a standalone balance.)

### 1.3 · Credit ownership
Credit belongs **only to the individual member**. It **cannot be transferred** to another member,
**shared** between family members, or **merged** into another account. Each member maintains an
**independent** financial ledger.

### 1.4 · Annual subscription model
The Food Fund applies **one annual subscription** to every eligible family member. The amount **may change
year to year**. Applying a new subscription simply **increases the running balance**; previous **credit
auto‑offsets** the new assessment; previous **debt remains** part of the same running balance.

### 1.5 · Cross‑program credit — Not Applicable (current scope)
The current constitutional scope contains **one financial program (Food Fund)**. **Cross‑program credit
(e.g., Food ↔ Diwan) is Not Applicable**, while **preserving the possibility of future constitutional
expansion** if additional financial programs are ever introduced. (Any prior example using Food↔Diwan credit
is illustrative only and does not reflect current scope.)

## 2 · Affected constitutional laws
- **Law 1 (Conservation)** — credit never vanishes; it is purchasing power that offsets future assessments.
- **Law 3 (Single Source of Truth)** — one independent running balance per member; no shared/merged ledgers.
- **Law 8 (Custody)** — cross‑program value movement is not permitted in current scope (reserved for a future amendment).
